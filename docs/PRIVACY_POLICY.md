# deadletter — Privacy Policy

## What We Collect

- **Message content**: Only the anonymized version is stored. Original messages are processed and immediately discarded.
- **Phone number hash**: We store a one-way SHA-256 hash of your phone number. We cannot reverse this to get your actual number.

## What We Do NOT Collect

- Your real phone number
- Your name
- Your location
- Your device information
- Any personal identifying information

## Anonymization Process

Every message goes through a 2-stage anonymization process before any data is stored:

1. **Stage 1 (Regex)**: Automatically removes phone numbers, email addresses, social security numbers, addresses, and credit card numbers.
2. **Stage 2 (AI)**: Claude AI reviews the text to identify and remove personal names, company names, school names, specific locations, and social media handles.

## Crisis Messages

Messages that contain crisis indicators (self-harm, suicide ideation) are:
- Responded to with professional crisis resources
- Saved privately and **never** published on the website

## Data Retention

- Anonymized letters: Stored indefinitely (published on website)
- Session data: Automatically cleared after 30 minutes of inactivity
- Phone hashes: Stored for session management only

For privacy concerns, please reach out via iMessage to our service number.
