# deadletter — Content Policy

## What Gets Published

- Anonymized personal stories, confessions, and thoughts
- Messages that pass both inbound and outbound content filters
- Messages where anonymization was fully successful

## What Does NOT Get Published

- **Crisis content**: Messages containing self-harm or suicide ideation
- **Spam**: Messages containing URLs, promotional content, or scam patterns
- **Over-redacted content**: Messages where more than 40% of words were redacted
- **Short messages**: Messages under 20 characters after anonymization
- **Blocked users**: Messages from users who have been blocked

## Moderation

### Inbound Filter
- Spam detection (URLs, promotional patterns)
- Message length validation (2–5000 characters)
- Blocked user check

### Outbound Filter
- Excessive redaction check
- Remaining PII detection (social handles, URLs)
- Minimum publishable length

## Reporting

If you find a published letter that contains identifiable information, please contact us immediately via iMessage. We will review and remove the content.
